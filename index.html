About

Hi there. I'm Jeremy. This section is a little bit about me.

The first thing you should know is that I got into development because I love it, and I figure if you want to be great at what you do, you better start by choosing what you love.

The second thing to note is that I chose Dev Bootcamp as deliberately as my career path. They encourage curiousity, passion-driven learning, and cultivation of emotional intelligence so that graduates are not only excellent learners, but great to work with. Considering what a pleasure it is working with my fellow boots, I'd say it works.

Other things I love: well, "free time" is a free-for-all of marathons, music, exploring, and mobilizing people.

Please check out my blog and projects page to see what I learned before arriving at Dev Bootcamp (with 80-100 hour weeks, I must apologize that I was not able to keep up with this blog during my time onsite!)
