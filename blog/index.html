About

Hi there. I'm Jeremy. This section is all about who I am.

The first thing you should know is that I got into development because I love it, and I figure if you want to be great at what you do, you better start by choosing what you love. That's why I left my career in biotech; I wasn't excited about it, and knew that I could find a career I was naturally more curious and passionate about. In tech, I will excel simply because it fascinates me.

The second thing is that I chose Dev Bootcamp as deliberately as my career path. They encourage a passion-driven curiosity for learning, and cultivation of emotional intelligence so that graduates are great learners and easy to work with. That's what I aim to be, and I'd love to show it off with this website.

Please check out my blog and projects page to see what I've learned so I can back up otherwise difficult-to-prove statements like "I'm a quick learner!"
