Hawk of Ragnar

Ragnar is wounded and needs to send out his hawk to gather food. His hawk is very smart, and can carry a basket with up to 2 rabbits in it. However, the basket is heavy, so the hawk can only carry it back toward Ragnar. Grab the basket, collect 2 rabbits, and bring them back to Ragnar to help him stay alive.
